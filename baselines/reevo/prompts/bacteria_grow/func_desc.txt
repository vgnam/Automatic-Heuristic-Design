The equation function takes as input numpy arrays representing bacterial population density (b), substrate concentration (s), temperature (temp), and pH level (pH), along with a params array containing up to 10 optimizable numerical coefficients (params[0] through params[9]), and returns a numpy array representing the predicted bacterial growth rate. You can use as few or as many parameters as needed for your equation design. Use only standard English letters and numbers. Do not output any special characters.
